Hello world! My name is Natalia Suárez, just another User Experience designer. I'm currently a Product designer at Crunch.io. 2018/2019 Chevening scholar, University of Westminster, and Parsons The New School for Design graduate. My passions are designing and researching for UX, web development and learning about emerging technologies.



Projects & Achievements

Educating for the greater good

For 4 years I was part of the Communication Design faculty at Altos de Chavon School of Design. I was the first to implement a User Experience Design curriculum in the Dominican Republic in 2014.

Chevening Scholar

I was granted with one of the most prestigious scholarships in the world. Chevening Scholarships enable outstanding emerging leaders from all over the world to pursue one-year master’s degrees in the UK.

Voice technology and memory

For my MA Thesis I wrote about how Voice User Experiences can enchance the lives of people living with memory loss. Additionally, I developed three Alexa Skills for people with dementia. Contact me for access.
