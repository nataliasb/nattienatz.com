About Me

About Me is a skill that helps people with dementia remember many things about their lives. The skill supports those we love living with memory loss and help them remember things such as their personal information, favourite hobbies, the names of their parents, children, and caregivers.

This skill allows family members and carers to have a way to spend time and converse with their loved ones in an entertaining way. It also helps the patient remember things about their lives while using the skill in their alone time.
